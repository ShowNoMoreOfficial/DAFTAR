## Instructions

### Brand Name
The Squirrels

### Client
Bhupendra Chaubey (veteran journalist)

### Channel
YouTube: The Squirrels (43K+ subscribers)

### Tagline
English political commentary with significant international audience

### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Analytical, measured, data-driven, intellectually provocative
- **Register**: Educated casual — like a smart professor at a coffee shop, not a newsroom
- **Language**: English (pure — no Hinglish for this brand)
- **Personality traits**: Intelligent, curious, fair-minded, occasionally contrarian, never preachy
- **What we sound like**: The Economist meets a thoughtful YouTube essayist
- **What we don't sound like**: News anchor, political pundit, angry commentator

### Editorial Guidelines
- **Topics we cover**: Geopolitics, Indian foreign policy, economics (macro), defense/security, technology policy, global trade, India's global positioning
- **Topics we avoid**: Celebrity gossip, Bollywood, sports, religion (unless geopolitically relevant), hyperlocal news
- **Position on controversy**: We analyze, we don't agitate. We can take positions, but they must be data-backed and diplomatically framed
- **Fact-checking standard**: Every claim must be traceable to the fact dossier. Minimum 2 sources per key claim.
- **Default diplomatic level**: Level 2 (Analyst) — explain implications without prescribing

### Visual Identity
- **Primary colors**: Navy blue (#1B3A5C), white, accent teal (#2E86AB)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, data-forward
- **Thumbnail style**: Clean, data-forward, face + bold stat + 3-word context. Professional, not sensational.
- **Logo usage**: Squirrel icon in corner of all thumbnails and graphics

### Content Strategy
- Primary format: YouTube explainers (10-15 min)
- Secondary: X threads (analytical), LinkedIn posts (professional framing)
- Posting cadence: 2-3 YouTube videos/week, daily X presence
- Target quality: Tier A minimum, Tier S monthly
