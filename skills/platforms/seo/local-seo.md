## Instructions

Optimize content for location-specific search queries, particularly for India's regional audiences. Local SEO for media brands focuses on geographic content targeting rather than Google Maps listings.

### Why Local SEO Matters for News/Analysis

- "UP election 2026" has different search behavior than "India election 2026"
- Regional queries often have less competition and more engaged audiences
- Hindi-belt audience searches in Hinglish — unique keyword opportunities
- Local/state politics coverage connects to national stories (audience retention driver)

### Regional Keyword Strategy

#### Geographic Modifiers
Add location terms to topic keywords:
- "[Topic] + India" (broad)
- "[Topic] + [State]" (regional: "budget impact UP", "election Rajasthan")
- "[Topic] + [City]" (local: "inflation impact Delhi", "startup ecosystem Bangalore")

#### Language-Based Targeting
| Audience | Search Language | Content Language |
|---|---|---|
| Hindi belt (UP, MP, Bihar, Rajasthan) | Hindi + Hinglish | Hindi/Hinglish |
| Metro India (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) | English + Hinglish | English |
| South India | English | English |
| Diaspora (US, UK, UAE) | English | English |

### Regional Content Types

#### State/Regional Analysis
- Connect national stories to state-level impact
- "Modi ki nayi policy — UP mein kya badlega?"
- Low competition, high relevance for target audience

#### Regional Comparison
- State vs state data comparisons
- "Bihar vs UP: Development index comparison 2026"
- Carousel/infographic format works well

#### Election Coverage by State
- State election content has massive search demand during election cycles
- Prepare pillar pages for upcoming state elections 3 months in advance
- Update frequently during campaign period

### Geo-Targeting on YouTube
- YouTube allows targeting by country and language preference
- Use Hindi titles/tags to capture Hindi-speaking audience regardless of location
- Diaspora audience searches in English for India topics — capture with English keywords

### Google Business Profile
- If the brand has a physical studio/office, maintain a Google Business Profile
- Helps establish local authority and appears in knowledge panels
- Not primary strategy but useful for brand credibility

### hreflang Implementation
- If website has Hindi and English versions of the same content:
  - `hreflang="hi"` for Hindi version
  - `hreflang="en"` for English version
- Ensures correct version appears for each user's language preference
