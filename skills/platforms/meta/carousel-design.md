## Instructions

Design carousel posts that deliver data-driven analysis in a swipeable, shareable format.

### Carousel Architecture

#### Slide Structure (8-10 slides optimal)
1. **Cover slide**: Bold hook — question or surprising claim. Brand colors, logo visible.
2. **Context slide**: Why this matters / what happened
3. **Data slides (3-5)**: One insight per slide. Chart, stat, or key point.
4. **Analysis slide**: What it means / implications
5. **Conclusion slide**: Bottom line / takeaway
6. **CTA slide**: "Save for later", "Share with someone who needs to see this", follow prompt, video link

#### Design Principles
- **One idea per slide** — don't cram. If it needs two sentences, split into two slides.
- **Large text**: Minimum 24pt equivalent — readable without zooming
- **Consistent template**: Same layout, fonts, colors across all slides
- **Progressive revelation**: Each slide should create curiosity for the next

### Brand-Specific Design

**The Squirrels**:
- Colors: Navy (#1B3A4B), teal (#2E86AB), white backgrounds
- Typography: Clean sans-serif, data-forward
- Style: Minimal, professional, chart-heavy
- Language: English

**Breaking Tube**:
- Colors: Red (#D32F2F), black, yellow accents
- Typography: Bold, impactful, large Hindi/English text
- Style: High-energy, text-overlay heavy
- Language: Hinglish — Hindi for emotion, English for data

### Content Types for Carousels
| Type | Slides | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Data breakdown | 8-10 | Budget analysis, economic data |
| Timeline/chronology | 6-8 | Event sequences, policy evolution |
| Comparison | 6-8 | India vs Pakistan, before vs after |
| Explainer | 8-10 | Complex policy made simple |
| Key quotes | 5-7 | Speech analysis, statement roundup |

### Caption Strategy
- First line: Hook that mirrors cover slide (appears above fold)
- Body: 2-3 sentences of additional context not on the carousel
- CTA: "Save this post", "Tag someone who should see this"
- Hashtags: 15-20 relevant hashtags (Instagram still indexes these)
- Line breaks between sections for readability

### Performance Optimization
- Carousels get 3x more engagement than single images on Instagram
- Slide 1 CTR determines reach — invest 50% of design effort on cover
- Swipe-through rate target: >50% reaching slide 3, >30% reaching final slide
- Save rate is the strongest signal for carousel distribution
