## Instructions

Build and manage Facebook Groups as owned-audience communities that supplement platform-dependent reach.

### Group Purpose by Brand

**The Squirrels Community**:
- Discussion forum for geopolitical analysis enthusiasts
- Audience shares articles, data, and asks analytical questions
- Moderated for quality — no low-effort posts
- English-primary discussion

**Breaking Tube Community**:
- Active discussion around daily political events
- Higher volume, more conversational
- Hinglish discussion encouraged
- Live reactions during events and video premieres

### Content Strategy for Groups

#### Daily Posting Schedule
- **Morning**: Discussion prompt — "Aaj ki sabse badi khabar kya hai aur kyun?"
- **Post-video**: Share video link with exclusive additional context not in the video
- **Evening**: Open thread for general discussion

#### Exclusive Content (Group-Only Value)
- Behind-the-scenes insights not shared publicly
- Early access to topic polls (what should we cover next?)
- Extended data/analysis that didn't fit in the video
- Direct responses to member questions from the host

### Moderation Framework

#### Rules
1. No personal attacks or hate speech
2. No misinformation — claims must have sources
3. No spam or self-promotion
4. Stay on topic (politics, economics, geopolitics)
5. Respect diverse viewpoints — debate ideas, not people

#### Moderation Actions
- Warning for first offense
- 24-hour mute for second offense
- Ban for hate speech, spam, or repeated violations
- Approve posts manually for first 2 weeks of a new member's membership

### Growth Tactics
- Promote group in YouTube video descriptions and end screens
- Pin group link in X/Twitter bio
- Run monthly "invite challenge" — members who invite quality members get recognition
- Cross-promote during YouTube premieres and X Spaces

### Metrics
- **Active members**: % posting or commenting in last 28 days (target: >30%)
- **Daily active users**: Consistent engagement (not just post-video spikes)
- **Post quality**: Ratio of substantive discussions to low-effort reactions
- **Growth rate**: Net new members per week
