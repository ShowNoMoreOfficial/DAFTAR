## Instructions

Use YouTube's Community tab to maintain audience engagement between video uploads and prime viewers for upcoming content.

### Post Types

#### 1. Video Teaser / Coming Soon
- Post 2-4 hours before video publish
- Create anticipation: "Aaj shaam 7 baje — Pakistan ka budget analysis 🔥" (Breaking Tube)
- Or: "New analysis dropping tonight — India's semiconductor strategy has a $62B problem" (Squirrels)
- Include a compelling image or thumbnail preview

#### 2. Poll
- Ask opinion on current topic: binary or multiple choice
- Use to gauge audience interest before producing content
- Polls get highest engagement rate of any community post type
- Example: "Next video topic — kya cover karein? A) Budget impact B) Election analysis C) Foreign policy"

#### 3. Behind the Scenes / Personal
- Humanizes the channel — show research process, studio, team
- Use sparingly (1-2x/week) — audience is here for content, not vlogs
- Works especially well for Breaking Tube (personality-driven brand)

#### 4. Quick Take / Text Post
- React to breaking news when a full video isn't ready yet
- Establishes presence and speed: "Just saw this — full analysis coming tonight"
- Keep under 500 characters — community posts aren't articles

#### 5. Image/Infographic
- Share a key data point or chart from an upcoming or recent video
- Must be readable on mobile (large text, high contrast)
- Brand colors and logo visible

### Posting Cadence
| Brand | Frequency | Best Times |
|---|---|---|
| The Squirrels | 3-5 posts/week | 12 PM, 6 PM IST |
| Breaking Tube | 5-7 posts/week | 11 AM, 5 PM, during events |

### Engagement Rules
- Reply to top comments on community posts (first 30 minutes)
- Pin a comment on each post to direct conversation
- Use community posts to drive viewers to new videos (post link in comments)
- Never ignore polls — always follow through with the winning option
