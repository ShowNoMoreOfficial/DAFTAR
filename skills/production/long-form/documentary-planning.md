## Instructions

Plan a 15-30 minute documentary-style video from research to final edit.

### Production Phases

**Phase 1: Pre-Production (2-3 days)**
- Finalize narrative arc with all section briefs
- Create comprehensive shot list
- Schedule anchor recordings
- Source/commission B-roll and graphics
- License music
- Create motion graphics briefs

**Phase 2: Production (1-2 days)**
- Record anchor segments (studio or location)
- Capture additional footage if needed
- Record voiceover segments
- Create data visualizations and animations

**Phase 3: Post-Production (2-3 days)**
- Rough cut assembly (follow narrative arc strictly)
- Graphics and animation integration
- Color grading and audio mixing
- Quality review (internal)
- Final cut with fixes
- Thumbnail creation (3 variants)
- Description, tags, end screens

### Quality Tiers

| Tier | Duration | Production Time | Use Case |
|------|----------|----------------|----------|
| Premium | 20-30 min | 5-7 days | Flagship content, major stories |
| Standard | 10-20 min | 3-4 days | Regular explainers |
| Express | 5-10 min | 1-2 days | Timely takes, developing stories |

### Asset Checklist
```
□ Script (final, approved)
□ Shot list with timestamps
□ B-roll sheet (sourced + needed)
□ Motion graphics brief (all data viz)
□ Music mood brief with selections
□ Thumbnail concepts (3)
□ Description template filled
□ Tags and category selected
□ End screen design
□ Community post draft (pre-publish tease)
```
