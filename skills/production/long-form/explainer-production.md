## Instructions

The explainer is the bread-and-butter format — 10-15 minutes, data-driven, visually rich.

### Explainer Formula

**Visual Style**: 40% anchor on camera, 30% data visualizations, 20% B-roll/maps, 10% text overlays

**Script Structure**:
- Every 60 seconds of script = ~150 words
- 12-minute explainer = ~1,800 words
- Each section of the narrative arc maps to a script section
- Include visual directions inline: `[SHOW: Chart of oil prices 2020-2026]`

**Production Brief Template**:
```
EXPLAINER BRIEF
Title: [Working title]
Brand: [The Squirrels / Breaking Tube]
Duration: [10-15 min]
Deadline: [Date + time]

NARRATIVE ARC:
[Paste from narrative-arc-construction output]

VISUAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Data visualizations: [list with descriptions]
- Maps: [list with descriptions]
- B-roll needed: [list with sources]
- Text overlays: [key stats to display]

AUDIO:
- Anchor recording: [sections list]
- Voiceover: [if different from anchor]
- Music: [mood brief]

TEAM ASSIGNMENTS:
- Script: [person/AI]
- Recording: [person]
- Editing: [person]
- Graphics: [person/AI]
- Review: [person]
```

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] Opening hook tested against hook-engineering skill output
- [ ] Every claim traceable to fact dossier
- [ ] Data visualizations accurate and readable at mobile resolution
- [ ] Audio levels consistent throughout
- [ ] No section exceeds 3 minutes without a visual change
- [ ] Ending has clear CTA and end screen
