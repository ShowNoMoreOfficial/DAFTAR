## Instructions

Turn raw footage into compelling shorts with minimal editing — speed and authenticity are the values.

### Best Raw Clip Types
- Political speeches with a powerful soundbite
- Press conference reactions (surprise, anger, humor)
- Event footage showing scale or emotion
- Behind-the-scenes moments (humanizing)
- Data reveals (live reaction to statistics)

### Production Rules
- Minimal editing — the raw quality IS the appeal
- Add context text overlay in first 2 seconds: "What [Person] just said about [Topic]"
- Add brand watermark
- Trim to the single most compelling 15-45 seconds
- Don't add music unless the raw audio is poor
- Caption is mandatory (accessibility + mute viewing)

### Raw Clip Checklist
- [ ] Is the audio clear enough to understand?
- [ ] Is the visual quality acceptable (not blurry/shaky)?
- [ ] Does the clip make sense without prior context?
- [ ] Is there a clear beginning and end (not mid-sentence cut)?
- [ ] Copyright/fair use cleared?
