## Instructions

Produce shorts using AI-generated voiceover (ElevenLabs or similar) when anchor recording isn't feasible.

### When to Use AI Voiceover
- Rapid turnaround (< 2 hours) where anchor isn't available
- Templated daily updates where consistent voice matters more than personality
- Experimental content testing new angles before committing to full production
- Breaking news shorts that need to go out immediately

### When NOT to Use AI Voiceover
- Flagship content (Tier S/A) — always use real anchor
- Opinion/editorial pieces — personality is critical
- Interviews or conversational content
- Content where the audience expects to see/hear the anchor

### Voice Configuration
- Match brand voice profile: tone, pace, energy level
- The Squirrels: Measured, authoritative, clear diction, moderate pace
- Breaking Tube: Energetic, slightly faster pace, Hinglish cadence
- Always disclose AI voiceover in video description (transparency builds trust)

### Production Notes
- AI voiceover + B-roll + text overlays = fully automated short
- Review every AI-generated voiceover before publish — check for mispronunciations (especially Indian names, Hindi words)
- Pair AI voice with strong text overlays — the text carries meaning if voice sounds off
- Keep AI voiceover shorts to 30-45 seconds — shorter duration hides any uncanny quality
