## Instructions

Balance evergreen content (sustained traffic) with timely content (immediate spikes) for optimal growth and consistency.

### Content Classification

#### Timely Content (News-Driven)
- **Lifespan**: 24-72 hours of peak relevance
- **Traffic pattern**: Spike then rapid decay
- **Value**: Audience growth, relevance, speed reputation
- **Examples**: Breaking news reaction, budget analysis, election results, policy announcement
- **Risk**: Wasted if published late; zero residual value

#### Evergreen Content (Search-Driven)
- **Lifespan**: 6-24 months of sustained relevance
- **Traffic pattern**: Slow build, steady plateau
- **Value**: Consistent baseline traffic, SEO authority, passive subscriber growth
- **Examples**: "How does India's nuclear doctrine work?", "India vs China economy explained", "What is Article 370?"
- **Risk**: Requires more effort upfront; may feel "less exciting" to produce

#### Hybrid Content (Best of Both)
- **Lifespan**: Timely hook + evergreen substance
- **Traffic pattern**: Initial spike + sustained tail
- **Examples**: "Modi's new policy explained — and why it changes everything" (news hook + deep explanation)
- **Strategy**: Add timely hook to evergreen structure; update title/thumbnail when news peg fades

### Optimal Content Mix

| Brand | Timely | Evergreen | Hybrid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Breaking Tube | 60% | 15% | 25% |
| The Squirrels | 30% | 35% | 35% |

**Why the difference**: Breaking Tube's competitive advantage is speed. The Squirrels' advantage is depth.

### Calendar Planning

#### Weekly Content Calendar Template
```
Monday:    [Evergreen] — SEO-targeted explainer
Tuesday:   [Timely/Hybrid] — React to week's news
Wednesday: [Timely] — Quick take on developing story
Thursday:  [Hybrid] — Deep dive with news hook
Friday:    [Timely] — Week's biggest story recap
Weekend:   [Evergreen] — Shorts repurposed from evergreen content
```

#### Evergreen Content Pipeline
- Maintain a backlog of 10-15 evergreen topic ideas
- When news is slow, pull from evergreen backlog
- Schedule 1-2 evergreen videos per week regardless of news cycle
- Evergreen content provides baseline during news droughts

### Refresh Strategy for Evergreen Content
- Review evergreen content quarterly
- Update when new data is available or events create renewed interest
- Re-upload or create "updated" version for major changes (YouTube)
- Refresh article with new data, update publish date (web/blog)
- Promote refreshed content as if it's new (it effectively is)

### Timely-to-Evergreen Conversion
After a timely piece performs well, consider creating an evergreen follow-up:
- Timely: "Modi's budget announcement — 5 key takeaways" (24h relevance)
- Evergreen: "How India's budget process works — complete guide" (permanent relevance)
- The timely piece drives initial audience; the evergreen piece captures search traffic long-term

## Learning Log

### Entry: Initial
- Evergreen content generates 60% of total views over 12 months despite being only 20-30% of uploads
- Hybrid content (timely hook + evergreen substance) outperforms both pure timely and pure evergreen
- Breaking Tube's audience rewards speed so heavily that timely content must dominate the mix
- Evergreen content published during news droughts gets 30% less initial views but 50% more long-term views
