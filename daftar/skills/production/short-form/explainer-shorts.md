## Instructions

Create 30-60 second explainer shorts that deliver one key insight with maximum impact.

### Short-Form Formula
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds (45s is the sweet spot)
- **One insight per short**: Never try to explain everything
- **Visual-first**: Text overlay + motion must carry meaning even on mute (85% of shorts are watched without sound initially)

### Script Template
```
[0:00-0:02] HOOK: Bold text overlay + movement. "India just lost ₹4.2 lakh crore"
[0:02-0:10] CONTEXT: Quick setup. "Here's what happened in 15 seconds..."
[0:10-0:35] THE INSIGHT: The one thing the viewer must understand
[0:35-0:45] TAKEAWAY: "This means X for you" + CTA text overlay
```

### Visual Rules for Shorts
- First frame must have text AND movement — static frames get scrolled past
- Text overlay: Maximum 8 words per screen, high contrast, large font
- Use visual transitions every 3-5 seconds to maintain attention
- Data should be animated (counting up, bar growing) not static
- Face on screen for at least 30% of the duration — builds trust and familiarity
- Vertical (9:16) format only — never crop from horizontal

### Repurposing from Long-form
When extracting shorts from explainer videos:
- Identify the single best "aha moment" — usually the revelation at the 7-minute mark
- Re-record the hook specifically for short-form (don't just clip)
- Add new text overlays optimized for vertical
- The short should make viewers want to watch the full video
