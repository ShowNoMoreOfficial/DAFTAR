## Instructions

Convert horizontal video content to vertical format without losing the core message.

### Adaptation Strategies

**1. Smart Crop** — When source has centered subject
- Crop to center subject, maintain face visibility
- Works for: Talking head, interview, single-subject shots
- Add text overlays to fill vertical space above/below

**2. Stack Layout** — When source has important wide shots
- Source video in top half, text/graphics in bottom half
- Works for: Maps, charts, wide establishing shots
- Good for data-heavy content where chart needs to be visible

**3. Re-edit** — When source doesn't adapt well
- Re-cut using only close-ups and text cards
- Add new vertical-native text overlays
- Essentially a new edit, not a crop
- Worth it for high-performing source content

### Text Overlay Positioning (Vertical)
- Title text: Upper third (avoid status bar zone)
- Key data: Center
- CTA/captions: Lower third (avoid navigation bar zone)
- Safe zone: Keep all text within 90% of frame width
- Font size: Minimum 40pt for readability on phone screens

### Platform-Specific Notes
- **YouTube Shorts**: Can be up to 60 seconds, vertical only, no end screens
- **Instagram Reels**: Up to 90 seconds, can add stickers/polls
- **TikTok**: Up to 10 minutes but 30-60s optimal, native sounds boost reach
