## Instructions

Quick takes are speed-optimized: 5-8 minutes, minimal graphics, strong opinion, fast turnaround.

### Quick Take Formula
- **Turnaround**: 2-6 hours from signal to publish
- **Style**: Mostly anchor-on-camera with 3-5 supporting visuals
- **Quality bar**: 80% — speed > polish, but facts must be 100% accurate
- **Length**: 5-8 minutes (never exceed 10)

### Minimal Asset Requirements
```
REQUIRED:
- Script (can be bullet points, not full prose)
- 1 thumbnail
- 3-5 supporting visuals (screenshots, simple charts, maps)
- Background music (from library, not custom)

OPTIONAL (if time allows):
- 1 custom data visualization
- B-roll clips (from archive)
- Text overlays for key stats
```

### Script Template for Quick Takes
```
[0:00-0:10] HOOK: [Bold statement or surprising data]
[0:10-1:00] WHAT HAPPENED: [Facts, fast, no filler]
[1:00-3:00] WHY IT MATTERS: [The angle - this is the core value]
[3:00-5:00] THE BIGGER PICTURE: [Connect to trends, implications]
[5:00-5:30] WHAT HAPPENS NEXT: [Prediction or "watch for..."]
[5:30-6:00] CLOSE: [CTA - subscribe, comment, share]
```

### Speed vs Quality Tradeoffs
- Skip: Custom animations, complex graphics, multiple camera angles
- Don't skip: Fact-checking, audio quality, thumbnail quality
- The thumbnail still needs to be excellent — it determines whether speed was worthwhile
