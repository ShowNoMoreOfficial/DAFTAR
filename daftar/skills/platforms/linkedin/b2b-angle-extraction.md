## Instructions

Extract business-relevant angles from political and economic content to make it resonate with LinkedIn's professional audience.

### The B2B Translation Framework

Every political/economic story has business implications. The job is to find and articulate them.

#### Translation Pattern
```
Political Event → Policy Impact → Industry Effect → Business Action
```

#### Example Translations

| Original Topic | B2B Angle |
|---|---|
| India imposes tariffs on Chinese electronics | What this means for Indian tech companies and their supply chains |
| Pakistan's budget allocates 40% to defense | Implications for India-Pakistan trade corridor and cross-border business |
| RBI changes interest rates | How this affects startup funding, real estate, and consumer spending |
| New data privacy regulation | What companies need to change in their data handling practices |
| Election results | Policy changes to expect and how to prepare your business |

### Industry Verticals to Target

#### Primary (Most Relevant to Content)
- **Technology**: Semiconductor policy, digital regulation, startup ecosystem
- **Finance**: Monetary policy, banking regulation, market impact
- **Defense/Aerospace**: Defense procurement, strategic partnerships
- **Energy**: Renewable policy, oil imports, energy security
- **Trade/Commerce**: Tariffs, FTAs, export policy

#### Secondary
- **Real Estate**: Interest rates, urbanization policy, infrastructure
- **Healthcare**: Policy changes, public health spending
- **Education**: EdTech policy, skill development programs
- **Agriculture**: Farm policy, MSP changes, agritech

### Content Extraction Process
1. **Identify the policy/event**: What happened?
2. **Map affected industries**: Who does this impact directly?
3. **Quantify the impact**: What are the numbers? (₹ value, % change, timeline)
4. **Translate to business action**: What should a professional in this industry DO with this information?
5. **Frame as LinkedIn insight**: "If you're in [industry], here's what [event] means for your [specific concern]"

### Writing the B2B LinkedIn Post
```
[Hook: Business-relevant statement]

[Context: What happened in 1-2 sentences]

[Impact: 3-4 bullet points on industry implications]

[Analysis: Your professional take — what's the non-obvious implication]

[Action: What should professionals do about this?]

[Question: Drive discussion from industry insiders]
```

### What NOT to Do
- Don't just repost political analysis with no business angle
- Don't take overtly political sides (LinkedIn audience wants analysis, not partisanship)
- Don't use casual/entertainment tone from other platforms
- Don't assume LinkedIn audience has deep political context — provide it
