## Instructions

Optimize LinkedIn Creator Mode features to maximize reach, follower growth, and content distribution.

### Creator Mode Features to Activate

#### 1. Newsletter
- **Priority**: HIGH — LinkedIn pushes newsletter content aggressively
- Subscribers get email + in-app notifications for every edition
- Naming: "The Squirrels Weekly Brief" / "Breaking Tube Insights"
- Cadence: Weekly or bi-weekly — consistency matters
- Content: Repurpose best analysis into newsletter format (not just video links)

#### 2. LinkedIn Live
- Available with Creator Mode enabled
- Use for: Breaking event analysis, Q&A sessions, panel discussions
- Cross-promote with X Spaces for simultaneous multi-platform live presence
- Notify subscribers before going live

#### 3. Featured Section
- Pin best-performing content at top of profile
- Rotate monthly to keep fresh
- Include: Best article, most viral post, key YouTube video link
- First impression for profile visitors — showcase strongest work

#### 4. Talk About Topics
- Select 5 topics that define your expertise
- These appear on your profile and help LinkedIn categorize your content
- The Squirrels: Geopolitics, Economics, India, Data Analysis, Foreign Policy
- Breaking Tube: Indian Politics, Current Affairs, Political Analysis, Policy, News

### Profile Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Professional headshot (or brand logo for brand pages)
- [ ] Headline with keywords: "Geopolitical Analyst | Data-Driven" not just "YouTuber"
- [ ] Banner image with brand identity
- [ ] Bio: 3-paragraph format — who, what, why follow
- [ ] Featured section: 3-5 best pieces
- [ ] Custom URL: linkedin.com/in/thesquirrels
- [ ] Creator mode ON with relevant topics selected

### Content Calendar for Creator Mode
| Day | Content Type | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Data post (chart + insight) | Start week with value |
| Tuesday | Article or Newsletter | Deep analysis |
| Wednesday | Video promotion | Drive to YouTube |
| Thursday | Opinion/take post | Engagement + comments |
| Friday | Industry reflection or question | Community building |

### Growth Tactics
- Comment on posts by larger creators in your niche (first 30 minutes of their post)
- Engage with every comment on your posts within 1 hour
- Connect (not just follow) with journalists, analysts, policymakers
- LinkedIn's algorithm heavily rewards daily posting consistency
