## Instructions

Decide when to use a LinkedIn post vs. a LinkedIn article, and how to optimize each format.

### Decision Matrix

| Criteria | Post | Article |
|---|---|---|
| Length | Under 1,500 characters | 800-2,000 words |
| Depth | Single insight, quick take | Deep analysis, full argument |
| Frequency | 3-5x/week | 1-2x/month |
| Reach | Higher (appears in feed natively) | Lower (requires click-through) |
| SEO | Minimal | Google-indexed, long-term traffic |
| Engagement | Comments, likes, shares | Reads, shares, newsletter signups |
| Best for | Timely reactions, data points, opinions | Evergreen analysis, reference content |

### When to Choose a Post
- Reacting to news within 24 hours
- Sharing a single data point or insight
- Promoting a YouTube video
- Starting a discussion / asking a question
- Building consistent presence (majority of content)

### When to Choose an Article
- Deep analysis that requires 800+ words and structure
- Content that should be Google-searchable long-term
- Building SEO authority on a topic
- Repurposing a YouTube explainer into written form
- Creating a reference piece ("The Complete Guide to India's Semiconductor Strategy")

### Post Optimization
- **Hook**: First 2 lines appear above "...see more" — these MUST hook the reader
- **Structure**: Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences), line breaks between points
- **CTA**: End with a question to drive comments
- **Image/Document**: Attach a carousel (PDF upload) or image for 2x engagement
- **No links in post body**: LinkedIn suppresses posts with external links — put links in first comment

### Article Optimization
- **Title**: SEO-optimized, keyword-rich (appears in Google search)
- **Cover image**: Professional, branded, relevant
- **Subheadings**: Every 200-300 words for scanability
- **Internal links**: Link to other LinkedIn articles or profile
- **Publish timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8 AM IST
- **Promote**: Share the article as a post for initial distribution

### Hybrid Strategy
Best approach: Publish an article, then create 3-4 posts that excerpt key insights from it, linking back to the article. This maximizes both formats.

## Learning Log

### Entry: Initial
- Posts get 5x more immediate reach than articles but articles accumulate views over months
- Posts with carousels (PDF upload) get highest engagement of any LinkedIn format
- Putting links in first comment instead of post body increases reach by 30-40%
- Articles published on Tuesday mornings get highest read-through rates
