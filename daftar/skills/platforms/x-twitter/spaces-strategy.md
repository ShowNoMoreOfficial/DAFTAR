## Instructions

Plan and execute X/Twitter Spaces as live engagement events that build community and generate repurposable content.

### When to Host Spaces

#### High-Value Triggers
- **Breaking event**: Major political/economic development — go live for real-time analysis
- **Post-video deep dive**: After a popular video, host Q&A Space for deeper discussion
- **Recurring series**: Weekly or bi-weekly Space on a fixed topic (builds habit)
- **Guest availability**: When a relevant expert/journalist agrees to co-host

#### When NOT to Use Spaces
- Topic is too niche (won't attract minimum viable audience)
- No time for promotion (minimum 24-hour lead time for planned Spaces)
- Competing with a much larger Space on same topic (get drowned out)

### Space Planning

#### Pre-Space (24-48 Hours Before)
1. Schedule Space on X with compelling title
2. Promote with 3-4 tweets spread across promotion window
3. Create community post / YouTube community tab announcement
4. Brief co-hosts/guests on format and talking points
5. Prepare 5-7 discussion questions as backup

#### During Space
- **Opening**: State the topic, introduce speakers, set ground rules (5 min)
- **Core discussion**: Structured but flexible — follow the energy (30-45 min)
- **Audience Q&A**: Bring listeners to speak (15-20 min)
- **Closing**: Summarize key points, plug upcoming content, thank participants

#### Duration
- **Optimal**: 45-75 minutes
- **Minimum**: 30 minutes (shorter feels rushed)
- **Maximum**: 90 minutes (attention drops off significantly after)

### Moderation Rules
- Mute disruptive speakers immediately
- Keep listener queue managed — don't let queue grow beyond 5
- If a listener goes off-topic, politely redirect or mute
- Have a co-host manage the technical side while main host focuses on content

### Brand-Specific Approach

**The Squirrels Spaces**:
- Analytical, structured format
- Data-driven discussion with slides/charts shared via tweets during Space
- Invite expert guests to add credibility
- English-primary

**Breaking Tube Spaces**:
- High-energy, opinion-forward discussion
- More audience interaction — bring more listeners to mic
- Hinglish conversation flows naturally
- Bhupendra's personality drives engagement

### Content Repurposing
- Record every Space (X auto-records for 30 days)
- Extract 3-5 best clips (60-90 seconds each) for tweets and Shorts
- Create a summary thread of key points discussed
- Use insights as basis for a follow-up video
