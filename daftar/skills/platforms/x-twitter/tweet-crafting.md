## Instructions

Craft tweets that maximize engagement (replies, retweets, quotes) while maintaining brand voice and credibility.

### Tweet Types

#### 1. Breaking Reaction (Speed-First)
- First to comment on breaking development
- Keep under 200 characters — fast to read, fast to share
- State the fact + add one analytical sentence
- Example (Squirrels): "India just imposed 25% tariffs on Chinese electronics. This changes the semiconductor calculus entirely. Here's what to watch."
- Example (Breaking Tube): "Modi ne abhi-abhi yeh announce kiya — iska seedha asar aapki jeb par padega. Details aa rahe hain."

#### 2. Data Bomb
- Single surprising data point with context
- Include a visual if possible (chart, infographic)
- Example: "Pakistan's defense budget: 40% of total spending. Education: 8%. One chart tells the whole story."

#### 3. Hot Take / Opinion
- Clear position on a debate — the audience follows for opinions
- Must be grounded in facts, not just rhetoric
- Example: "Everyone's celebrating 7% GDP growth. Nobody's asking why rural consumption is down 12%. The data tells a different story."

#### 4. Video Promotion
- Promote YouTube video with a compelling hook (not just "new video out")
- Include the best insight from the video as the hook
- Add video link — Twitter shows preview card
- Example: "India spends $38B annually on something it can't make. We did the math on what it would take to change that. [link]"

#### 5. Engagement Bait (Strategic)
- Questions, polls, "what do you think?" — drives replies
- Use sparingly (2-3x/week) — too much feels like engagement farming
- Example: "Sabse bada election issue 2026 mein kya hoga? Reply kariye."

### Character Strategy
- **Under 100 chars**: Maximum shareability — punchy one-liners
- **100-200 chars**: Sweet spot for most tweets — statement + context
- **200-280 chars**: Full analysis micro-take — use when nuance matters
- **Never**: Hit exactly 280 — looks like you're straining to fit

### Voice Calibration

**The Squirrels on X**:
- Slightly more casual than YouTube — platform demands it
- Can use "I" more (personal voice acceptable on X)
- Data-first, but with personality
- No emojis in analytical tweets (professional register)

**Breaking Tube on X**:
- Full Hinglish — matches audience
- High energy, opinion-forward
- Can be more direct/punchy than YouTube
- Emojis acceptable for emphasis (🔥, ⚡, 🧵)

### Posting Rules
- Never tweet the same content twice (even rephrased) within 48 hours
- Space tweets minimum 30 minutes apart (don't flood timeline)
- Quote tweet > retweet when adding analysis (more visible, more engagement)
- Always reply to high-engagement comments within first hour
