## Instructions

Optimize YouTube Shorts as the primary discovery and subscriber acquisition channel.

### Shorts Architecture

#### The 1.5-Second Rule
First 1.5 seconds determine if viewer stays or swipes. Must include:
- **Text overlay**: Bold, readable statement or question
- **Face/movement**: Human element creates connection
- **Visual change**: Cut, zoom, or motion — static = death

#### Content Types for Shorts
1. **Clip extraction**: Best 30-60s from long-form video (primary strategy)
2. **Standalone take**: Quick opinion on trending topic (speed-first)
3. **Data punch**: Single surprising statistic with context (works for Squirrels)
4. **Reaction clip**: Response to viral clip or statement (works for Breaking Tube)

#### Structure Template (45-60 seconds)
```
[0-1.5s] HOOK — text overlay + face + energy
[1.5-15s] CONTEXT — what's this about, why care
[15-40s] SUBSTANCE — the actual insight/data/take
[40-55s] PAYOFF — the conclusion or surprising twist
[55-60s] CTA — "Full video link in description" or "Follow for more"
```

### Brand-Specific Rules

**The Squirrels Shorts**:
- Clean, minimal text overlays (white on dark, sans-serif)
- Data-driven hooks: "India imports $38B worth of chips every year"
- Measured pace — don't force energy that doesn't match the brand
- English text overlays

**Breaking Tube Shorts**:
- Bold, colorful text overlays (red, yellow — brand colors)
- High energy from frame 1 — match Bhupendra's natural delivery
- Hindi text overlays outperform English by 40%
- Reaction-style and opinion-first formats work best

### Shorts SEO
- Title: 40-50 characters max (limited display)
- Include #Shorts in description (not strictly required but helps)
- Add 2-3 relevant hashtags
- Description: 1-2 sentences + link to full video

### Repurpose Strategy
- Every long-form video should yield 2-3 Shorts
- Extract different angles, not just "best moment"
- Shorts posted same day as long-form create discovery funnel:
  Short → viewer clicks profile → sees long-form → watches

### Posting Cadence
- **Minimum**: 5-7 Shorts/week per brand
- **Optimal**: 1-2 Shorts/day
- **Best times**: 12 PM, 3 PM, 7 PM IST (multiple daily slots)
- Don't post Shorts within 1 hour of long-form publish (they compete)
