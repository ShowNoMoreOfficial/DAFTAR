## Instructions

Conduct keyword research to identify search demand and optimize content for organic discovery across Google, YouTube, and social platforms.

### Research Process

#### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Start with 3-5 seed keywords from the topic:
- The topic itself: "india semiconductor policy"
- Related terms: "india chip manufacturing", "semiconductor india 2026"
- Question variants: "why india can't make chips", "india vs china semiconductors"

#### Step 2: Expand Using Sources
- **YouTube Autocomplete**: Type seed keyword in YouTube search — suggestions show real demand
- **Google Autocomplete**: Same approach, different audience intent
- **People Also Ask**: Google's PAA boxes reveal question-format demand
- **Related Searches**: Bottom of Google results page
- **Competitor analysis**: What keywords do top-ranking competitors target?

#### Step 3: Categorize Keywords
| Category | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Informational | User wants to learn | "what is india semiconductor policy" |
| Navigational | User looking for specific content | "breaking tube pakistan budget" |
| Transactional | User wants to act | Less relevant for news content |
| Current event | Time-sensitive searches | "india china border 2026" |

#### Step 4: Prioritize
Score each keyword on:
- **Search volume**: How many people search this (high = good)
- **Competition**: How many quality results exist (low = opportunity)
- **Relevance**: How well does this match our content and audience (high = essential)
- **Timeliness**: Is search volume growing or declining (growing = prioritize)

### Platform-Specific Keyword Research

#### YouTube Keywords
- YouTube search is the second-largest search engine
- Focus on: How-to, explanations, reactions, analysis keywords
- Video-intent keywords: "explained", "analysis", "reaction", "in hindi"
- Check YouTube Studio's search insights for audience search terms

#### Google Keywords (for Articles/Blog)
- Longer keywords (4+ words) have lower competition
- Focus on question keywords for featured snippets
- News keywords spike during events — prepare content in advance

#### Social Platform Keywords
- Instagram: Hashtag volume and related hashtags
- LinkedIn: Trending topics and industry buzzwords
- X/Twitter: Trending hashtags and search autocomplete

### Keyword Mapping to Content
```
Primary Keyword → Title (YouTube/Article)
Secondary Keywords → Description, tags, subheadings
Long-tail Keywords → Specific sections, FAQ content
Question Keywords → Video chapters, social posts
```

### Competitive Gap Analysis
1. Identify top 5 competitors for your topic
2. List keywords they rank for that you don't
3. Assess: Can we create better content for these keywords?
4. Prioritize gaps where our brand has a unique angle
