## Instructions

Configure and optimize Meta (Instagram + Facebook) advertising campaigns for audience growth and content promotion.

### Campaign Objectives by Goal

| Goal | Meta Objective | Optimization |
|---|---|---|
| Subscriber growth | Traffic (to YouTube) | Link clicks |
| Video views | Video views | ThruPlay (15s+) |
| Brand awareness | Reach | Impressions |
| Page/profile growth | Engagement | Page likes / profile visits |
| Community building | Traffic (to Group) | Link clicks |

### Audience Targeting

#### The Squirrels Audiences
- **Core**: 25-45, English-speaking, interests: geopolitics, economics, foreign policy, data journalism
- **Lookalike**: Based on YouTube subscribers or website visitors
- **Geography**: India (metros), US, UK, Singapore, UAE (diaspora)
- **Exclusions**: Existing subscribers/followers (avoid paying for existing audience)

#### Breaking Tube Audiences
- **Core**: 22-40, Hindi-speaking, interests: Indian politics, news, current affairs, Modi, BJP, Congress
- **Lookalike**: Based on YouTube subscribers or Facebook page engagement
- **Geography**: India (Hindi belt: UP, MP, Rajasthan, Bihar, Delhi NCR + metros)
- **Exclusions**: Existing subscribers/followers

### Creative Best Practices
- **Video ads**: First 3 seconds must hook — use best clip from content
- **Image ads**: Thumbnail-style with bold text, brand colors
- **Carousel ads**: Data-driven carousel repurposed from organic content
- **Copy**: Short, punchy — match organic brand voice
- **CTA button**: "Watch More" for video, "Learn More" for articles, "Join Group" for community

### Budget Allocation
- **Testing phase**: ₹500-1000/day split across 3-4 ad sets
- **Scaling phase**: Increase budget 20% every 3 days for winning ad sets
- **Kill threshold**: If CPC > ₹15 or CPV > ₹3 after 3 days, kill the ad set
- **Rule of thumb**: 70% budget on proven audiences, 30% on testing new ones

### Metrics & Optimization
- **Primary**: Cost per subscriber/follower acquired
- **Secondary**: CPC (cost per click), CPV (cost per view), CTR
- **Review**: Daily for first week, then 3x/week for ongoing campaigns
- **A/B test**: Always run 2-3 creative variants per ad set
