## Instructions

Optimize Instagram content for search discovery. Instagram's search has evolved significantly — treat it as a search engine, not just a social feed.

### Instagram Search Ranking Factors

#### Text Signals
- **Username**: Include primary keyword in name field (not handle). "The Squirrels | Geopolitics & Data" or "Breaking Tube | Political Analysis Hindi"
- **Bio**: Keyword-rich bio description
- **Caption keywords**: Instagram indexes caption text for search — use target keywords naturally
- **Hashtags**: Still relevant for categorization and discovery (though less than before)
- **Alt text**: Custom alt text is indexed — always write descriptive alt text

#### Engagement Signals
- Posts with high engagement rank higher in search results for related keywords
- Saves and shares are weighted most heavily
- Profile visits from search indicate relevance (self-reinforcing loop)

### Caption SEO Strategy

#### Keyword Placement
- Primary keyword in first line of caption (above the fold)
- 2-3 secondary keywords naturally in caption body
- Don't keyword-stuff — readability always wins over optimization
- Example: "India's defense budget just hit a record $74B. Here's what the numbers mean for the Indo-Pacific balance of power."

#### Hashtag SEO
- **Mix sizes**: 5 hashtags with 100K-500K posts (medium competition) + 5 with 10K-100K (low competition) + 5 niche/branded
- **Relevance over size**: #IndianDefenseBudget (10K posts) > #trending (500M posts)
- **Rotate hashtag sets**: Don't use identical hashtags every post (Instagram may flag as spam)
- **Placement**: In caption body or first comment — both work equally now

### Alt Text Optimization
- Write custom alt text for every image/carousel slide
- Describe the visual content AND include keywords
- Example: "Bar chart showing India's defense budget growth from 2020 to 2026, reaching $74 billion, compared to Pakistan and China"
- Instagram's auto-generated alt text is poor — always override

### Profile SEO
- **Name field**: "[Brand Name] | [Primary Keyword]" — this is searchable
- **Bio**: Include 3-4 keywords that describe your content
- **Category**: Select the most relevant business category
- **Highlights**: Name highlights with keywords (not just emojis)

### Reels SEO
- Caption: Include target keywords (Reels captions are indexed)
- On-screen text: Instagram can read text in videos — include keywords visually
- Audio: If using original audio, spoken keywords may be transcribed and indexed
- Cover image text: Readable, keyword-rich text on Reel covers
