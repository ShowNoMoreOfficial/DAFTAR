## Instructions

Produce Reels optimized for Instagram and Facebook's algorithm and audience behavior.

### Reel Architecture

#### Duration Strategy
- **15-30 seconds**: Highest completion rate, best for discovery
- **30-60 seconds**: Balanced — substance + retention
- **60-90 seconds**: Only for compelling stories that hold attention throughout
- **Default**: 30-45 seconds for most content

#### Structure Template
```
[0-2s] HOOK — text overlay + movement + face (stop the scroll)
[2-10s] SETUP — what's this about, why should I care
[10-35s] SUBSTANCE — the insight, data, or take
[35-45s] PAYOFF — conclusion + CTA text overlay
```

### Content Types
1. **Clip extraction**: Best moment from YouTube video — add text overlays, reframe vertical
2. **Talking head**: Direct-to-camera take with text overlay reinforcement
3. **Data visualization**: Animated charts/stats with voiceover
4. **News reaction**: Quick opinion on trending topic with B-roll

### Platform-Specific Optimization

#### Instagram Reels
- Cover image matters — it appears on profile grid. Design a custom cover or select best frame.
- Caption: Up to 2,200 characters but first 2 lines are crucial (above fold)
- Hashtags: 15-20 relevant tags in caption
- Music/audio: Trending audio gets algorithmic boost — use if it fits naturally
- Share to Feed AND Reels tab for maximum distribution

#### Facebook Reels
- Cross-post Instagram Reels to Facebook automatically
- Facebook audience skews slightly older — adjust tone if needed
- Facebook Reels get massive organic reach (Meta is pushing the format)
- Vertical format (9:16) required

### Brand-Specific Rules

**The Squirrels Reels**:
- Clean, minimal text overlays (white on navy/teal)
- Data-driven hooks
- Professional pace — don't force TikTok energy
- English captions and overlays

**Breaking Tube Reels**:
- Bold red/yellow text overlays
- High energy from frame 1
- Hindi text overlays — match YouTube Shorts approach
- Bhupendra's personality is the star — leverage face time

### Audio Strategy
- **Original audio**: Best for branded content, talking head pieces
- **Trending audio**: Use ONLY if it genuinely fits — forced trending audio hurts credibility
- **Voiceover**: Works well for data visualization reels
- **No audio (text-only)**: Can work but lower engagement — most users watch with sound

### Performance Metrics
- **Completion rate**: Primary metric — aim for >40% watching to end
- **Saves**: Strongest signal for algorithmic distribution
- **Shares**: Key growth metric — content shared to DMs/Stories
- **Profile visits**: Measures interest in more content from you
