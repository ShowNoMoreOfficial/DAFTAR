## Instructions

Use Stories as a real-time, always-on engagement channel that keeps the brand visible between main posts.

### Story Content Types

#### 1. Content Teaser
- Preview upcoming YouTube video or post
- Behind-the-scenes of research/production
- "Coming tonight at 7 PM — aaj ka analysis 🔥"
- Use countdown sticker for scheduled content

#### 2. Quick Reaction
- Breaking news reaction before full video is ready
- Text on branded background + 2-3 sentences
- Establishes speed and relevance

#### 3. Poll / Question
- Binary polls on trending topics: "Kya yeh sahi faisla hai? Yes / No"
- Question sticker: "What should we cover next?"
- Quiz sticker for audience education moments
- Polls get highest Story engagement rate

#### 4. Data Card
- Single stat or chart shared as Story slide
- Clean, readable at Story dimensions (1080x1920)
- Swipe-up / link sticker to full analysis

#### 5. Reshare
- Reshare mentions, good audience replies, or relevant content from others
- Builds community and rewards engagement
- Keep to 1-2 reshares per day (don't fill Stories with others' content)

### Story Sequence Strategy
- **Morning (8-10 AM)**: News of the day teaser or poll
- **Midday (12-2 PM)**: Data card or quick reaction
- **Evening (5-7 PM)**: Content teaser for tonight's video
- **Night (9-11 PM)**: Post-publish: "Video is live!" with link sticker
- **During events**: Real-time updates, 3-5 Stories per hour

### Highlights Strategy
Create permanent Story Highlights for key categories:
- "Latest" — most recent video/analysis
- "India-Pak" — all India-Pakistan coverage
- "Budget 2026" — event-specific highlights
- "About" — channel intro, what to expect
- Update Highlights regularly — stale Highlights signal inactive account

### Interactive Elements
| Sticker | When to Use | Engagement Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Poll | Daily — gauge audience opinion | Very High |
| Question | 2-3x/week — audience input | High |
| Quiz | Weekly — educate on topic | Medium |
| Countdown | Before video/Space/event | Medium |
| Link | Every video publish + key articles | Medium |
| Emoji slider | Fun/light topics only | Low |

### Performance Metrics
- **Reach**: % of followers seeing Stories
- **Tap forward rate**: High = content isn't compelling enough per slide
- **Tap back rate**: High = content IS compelling (they want to re-read)
- **Exit rate**: Where people leave the Story sequence
- **Sticker interaction rate**: % engaging with polls/questions
